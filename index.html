BUY A BOOK

The Damned Hero is a historical novel with elements of horror and fiction set in the early 18th century in Croatia, more precisely Zagreb, Varazdin and their surroundings. Mirela Pilko connects historical figures and places with her fictional world, telling a story of loss and distrust, spiced up with a dark plot. Allow her characters to take you into the world of vampires, demons and those who hunt them, which will make you begin to doubt your judgment, moral principles and the difference between good and evil.

BIO

Mirela grew up in Zagreb, Croatia, with a lot of time on her hands and a big imagination. With her nose buried in books or over the paper with a pencil in hand, drawing all those imaginary characters and worlds. She wrote her first story in primary school, something about pirates, flaying sharks and king Luis XIV. Incredibly diverse themes, nevertheless that was hers imagination.

Beside drawing and writing Mirela loves photography, forests, night creatures and moon. Her favourite themes are everything eerie, dark and mysterious. Mirela pretended to be a normal adult by holding down jobs that included customer service representative, bartender, checkout operator, and even a book seller in a book store. Mirela enjoys spending time with her teen nieces but her greatest joy is her son.
